This is the official WooCommerce extension to receive payments using the SmartPayLive payment gateway.

== Description ==

The SmartPayLive extension for WooCommerce enables you to accept Instant EFT payments via SmartPayLive payment gateway directly into your bank account.

You can setup a account with [SmartPayLive](https://smartpaylive.com) easily and start collecting payments in no time.

= WHY CHOOSE SMARTPAYLIVE ? =

SmartPayLive has no setup fees*, no monthly fee*, no hidden costs. you only get charged when you earn money! Earnings are transferred to your bank account directly from your customer's account.

[Signup](https://smartpaylive.com) now and start collecting via a World-Class, Innovative, Direct Merchanting Bank to Bank Instant EFT Payment Solution in South Africa.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Does this require a SmartPayLive merchant account? =

Yes! A SmartPayLive merchant account, channel name and channel password are required for this gateway to function.

= Does this require an SSL certificate? =

An SSL certificate is recommended for additional safety and security for your customers.
